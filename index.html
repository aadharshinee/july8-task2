Butter Chicken Recipe

Butter chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a rich and creamy Indian dish. Here are the common ingredients for preparing butter chicken:

Main Ingredients

  • Chicken: 500 grams, boneless and cut into pieces.

Marinade

  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup.
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 2 tablespoons.
  • Red Chili Powder: 1 teaspoon.
  • Turmeric Powder: 1/2 teaspoon.
  • Garam Masala Powder: 1 teaspoon.
  • Salt: to taste.
  • Lemon Juice: 1 tablespoon.

Sauce

  • Butter: 3 tablespoons.
  • Oil: 1 tablespoon.
  • Onions: 2 large, finely chopped.
  • Tomatoes: 3 large, pureed.
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 2 teaspoons.
  • Green Chilies: 1-2, slit.
  • Red Chili Powder: 1 teaspoon.
  • Coriander Powder: 1 teaspoon.
  • Garam Masala Powder: 1 teaspoon.
  • Cumin Powder: 1/2 teaspoon.
  • Fenugreek Leaves (Kasuri Methi): 1 teaspoon, crushed.
  • Cream: 1/2 cup.
  • Salt: to taste.
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon (optional, to balance the acidity of the tomatoes).

Garnish

  • Fresh Coriander Leaves: for garnishing.
  • Cream: for drizzling on top.

Method Summary

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Combine chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, salt, and lemon juice. Marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight in the refrigerator.
  2. Cook the Chicken: In a pan, heat some oil and cook the marinated chicken pieces until they are slightly charred and cooked through. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Sauce: In the same pan, add butter and a bit of oil. Sauté the finely chopped onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies, and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add Spices: Add red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and garam masala powder. Cook for a few seconds.
  5. Add Tomato Puree: Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts to separate from the sauce.
  6. Simmer: Add cooked chicken pieces to the sauce. Mix well and simmer for a few minutes.
  7. Finish the Sauce: Add cream and crushed fenugreek leaves (Kasuri Methi). Stir well and let it simmer for a couple more minutes. Adjust salt and add sugar if needed.
  8. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and a drizzle of cream. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
